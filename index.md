## About Me

I am a second year PhD student in Robotics at Colorado School of Mines, advised by [Prof. Yangming Shi](https://shiyangming.com/). My research focuses on quadruped robot navigation in complex and unstructured environments.

## Research Interests

- **Reinforcement Learning:** robot locomotion, robot navigation
- **Autonomous Navigation:** perception, prediction, path planning, end-to-end
